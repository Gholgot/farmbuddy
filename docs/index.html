FarmBuddy

Smart mount farming recommendations, scoring, and session planning for World of Warcraft.

Download Latest FarmBuddy.zip — extract to Interface/AddOns/ View on GitHub →

Installation

  1. Click Download Latest above to get FarmBuddy.zip.
  2. Extract the zip — you'll get a FarmBuddy/ folder.
  3. Move the FarmBuddy/ folder into your WoW AddOns directory:
    World of Warcraft/_retail_/Interface/AddOns/
  4. Launch (or reload) World of Warcraft. Type /farmbuddy in chat to open.

Features

Mount farming recommendations ranked by score
Scoring based on drop rate, time investment, and difficulty
Weekly lockout tracker across all your characters
Session planner to prioritize your farm list
Progress tracking by expansion
Mount search with filters and detailed stats
Achievement-based mount tracking
WoWHead links for every mount
